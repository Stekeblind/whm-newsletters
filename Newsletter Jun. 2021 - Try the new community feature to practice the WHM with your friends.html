App Community Feature
One of the most oft-heard requests for the WHM app is some way to be more closely connected to your fellow Wim Hof Method practitioners.

This month, we are introducing a brand new community feature that allows you to do just that: add your friends, track their exercises in real time, and cheer them on!

Take a sneak peek

Send someone a virtual hug in the blissful wake of their breathing session, or give them props for powering through that ice bath.

Please do note that this feature is not intended for you to try to beat your friends’ retention or cold shower times. The idea is to be communal and keep each other motivated.

This is our first crack at a feature like this. Things will be tweaked and added to foster a greater sense of community with each step. Thank you to all our subscribers for making this possible!
With the new update coming this month, you’ll also notice a new layout for the app. The main tab will now have an audio section, where you will find two more new subscription features:

In The Iceman Speaks, Wim narrates his latest book, The Wim Hof Method. Listen to the bitesize chapters on your daily commute, during a breathing session, or whenever you need to tune out the noise.

Last but very far from least, this is also where you will find our very own podcast: The Wim Hof Podcast, where Wim interviews fascinating folks who intersect with our philosophy in one way or another. The first episode will be available June 14, with Russell Brand as the inaugural guest. Save the date!
