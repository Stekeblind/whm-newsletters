Teeming Up
When Teemill told us about their approach to textile manufacturing, we were very eager to partner with them.

Just like we are in the business of recycling air, Teemill repurposes all the natural materials they use to make clothes. To give you a sense of just how far they push the definition of sustainability: the fabric is spun from organic cotton, and the leftover seeds are then pressed into cakes to feed cows. How cool is that?

Last month we took part in their ‘One Tee One Kg’ campaign, during which for every order placed with us, a kilogram of ocean-bound plastic was recovered and recycled. The recent Netflix documentary Seaspiracy was an incisive reminder that marine life is in dire straits, and you might just see Wim interview the director in the near future.

Teemill have considered their environmental impact at every step of the journey of your future hoodie, including manufacturing, land cultivation, and energy production. Going forward, they will be handling production of all our apparel, and we couldn’t be more excited.

We promptly filled the new shop with a bunch of new designs.
